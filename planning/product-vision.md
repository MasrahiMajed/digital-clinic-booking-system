- Product Vision

- Project Title
Digital Clinic Booking System

- Sustainable Development Goal
SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-Being

- Problem Statement
Students often experience long waiting times when visiting the university clinic. This causes inconvenience and wastes time.

- Proposed Solution
The Digital Clinic Booking System allows students to book clinic appointments online, view available time slots, and manage their appointments more easily.

- Target Users
University students
Clinic staff
Clinic administrators

- Key Features
Online appointment booking
View available appointment slots
Cancel or reschedule appointments
Manage bookings through a staff dashboard

- Expected Benefits
The system helps reduce waiting time, improves clinic efficiency, and gives students easier access to clinic services.
